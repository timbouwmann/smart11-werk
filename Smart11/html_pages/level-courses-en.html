Level 1
Content
Module 1
Introduction to the Methodology

Introduction to analyzing players individually.

Foundation of the Smart11 methodology:

  • Game Actions
  • Football roles
  • Decisions and execution of individual actions

Practical:

  • Working with SmartCoach
  • How to create and analyze clips
Goal: you understand how we look at football
Module 2
Match Themes & Game Actions

You learn to recognize and understand optimal responses in the most important match situations across 4 themes:

  • Build-up
  • 1v1 defending & attacking
  • Depth defending & attacking
  • Defending & attacking crosses

For each theme you learn:

  • What is the player's objective
  • What is the maximum result
  • Which decisions players make in that situation
  • What often goes wrong (and why)
  • How players can execute optimally
Goal: you understand how players can optimally respond to the most recurring match situations
Module 3
From Analysis to Training

Now you apply it on the pitch.

  • How to create mini-lessons per situation
  • How to translate analysis into concrete behavior
  • Concrete training exercises (PDFs) per theme
  • Demo: how to teach players to analyze themselves
Goal: you know how to analyze, make it trainable, and give players ownership over their development
Result after Level 1
You understand the power of analyzing players individually
You can create individual analyses using SmartCoach
You recognize the key individual football actions (Game Actions)
You know how to translate analysis to the training pitch
You understand the objective and maximum result per Game Action
You can use mini-lessons to quickly teach players a specific theme
You see the decisions players make and where things often go wrong
You can explain to players how to analyze themselves
Level 2
Content
Module 1
Advanced Match Theme Analysis

You deepen your understanding of all Level 1 themes:

  • Build-up
  • 1v1 defending & attacking
  • Depth defending & attacking
  • Defending & attacking crosses

You learn:

  • To recognize situations earlier
  • All preparatory Game Actions
  • To identify patterns in player behavior
  • To see where things structurally go wrong
Goal: you quickly recognize recurring patterns per position
Module 2
Position-Specific Analysis

You learn to analyze decisions and behavior per position:

  • Centre-back
  • Fullback
  • Midfielder
  • Winger
  • Striker

You learn:

  • What typical behavior looks like per position
  • What happens when players display certain behaviors
  • To recognize interactions (e.g. centre-back + midfielder, fullback + winger)
  • How one player's decisions affect others
You move from individual moments to understanding behavior and interaction
Module 3
From Analysis to Concrete Advice

You turn patterns into clear improvements.

  • Translate patterns into concrete insights
  • Determine the most important improvement point
  • Formulate clear advice for the player or (performance) coach

You learn:

  • This is happening → this is how to fix it
  • How to communicate this simply and clearly
  • How to use analysis within performance coaching
Goal: you create analyses that lead to directly applicable advice
Result after Level 2
You recognize patterns in player behavior
You can translate analysis into clear and practical advice
You understand what is happening per position and in interactions
You coach more effectively on what truly matters
Level 3
Content
Module 1
From Analysis to Development Plan

You learn how to turn an analysis (e.g. from Level 2) into a focused development plan per player.

You learn:

  • How to determine the most important improvement point
  • How to set priorities based on impact
  • What to focus on and what not
  • How long to work on specific areas
Goal: you turn analysis into a structured development cycle instead of loose insights
Module 2
Building an Individual Development Path

You learn how to guide a player within a structured trajectory.

  • Define focus together with the player
  • Set up a clear trajectory (e.g. 4–6 weeks)
  • Bring structure into development

You learn:

  • How to involve players in their own development
  • How to set clear expectations
  • How to track progress
Goal: you guide players in a structured development process with clear focus, purpose, and timeline
Module 3
Performance Coaching & Sessions

You learn how to guide players at a high level and make them more independent.

  • Build and deliver 1-on-1 sessions
  • Work with players who analyzed themselves or were analyzed by you
  • Help players reflect on their own behavior
  • Connect development to data and performance
  • Make progress visible
Goal: you can run effective 1-on-1 sessions, stimulate reflection, and guide players toward independence and better performance
Result after Level 3
You turn analyses into structured development plans
You run effective 1-on-1 sessions
You guide players through clear development trajectories
You work with both self-analyzing players and your own analyses
You connect development to measurable progress (data)
